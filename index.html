About me

The person behind the work

Two years in. Thirty-plus client projects. Still learning every day — and that's exactly how I like it.

Between October 2023 and June 2024 I completed several professional courses in Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Figma — building a strong technical foundation across the full design stack.

I then took that foundation straight into the real world on Fiverr, where clients don't wait and feedback is instant. That combination of structured learning and live client pressure taught me more than either could have alone.

Now I'm looking for a studio where I can keep growing alongside people who take their craft seriously.
